📋 CORPORATE PROFILE

About MKC Bank

A pioneering institution born from the ambition to create a smarter, more accessible banking experience for every Filipino.

(d) History & Background

MKC Bank Philippines, Inc. was established in 2025 by three young and ambitious Filipino entrepreneurs — Mike Jason Monte, Christian H. Balibagoso, and Christian H. Balibagoso — who shared a common vision: to build a bank that truly serves the modern Filipino. Headquartered in Makati City, the bank launched with an initial capitalization of ₱12 Billion and quickly gained BSP approval as a Universal Bank. Within its first year, MKC Bank opened 50 branches across Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao, with aggressive expansion plans to reach 200 branches by 2028.

(e) Subsidiaries
