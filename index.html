📋 CORPORATE PROFILE

About MKC Bank

A pioneering institution born from the ambition to create a smarter, more accessible banking experience for every Filipino.

History & Background

MKC Bank Philippines, Inc. was established in 2025 by three young and ambitious Filipino entrepreneurs — Mike Jason Monte, Christian H. Balibagoso, and Christian H. Balibagoso — who shared a common vision: to build a bank that truly serves the modern Filipino. Headquartered in Makati City, the bank launched with an initial capitalization of ₱12 Billion and quickly gained BSP approval as a Universal Bank. Within its first year, MKC Bank opened 50 branches across Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao, with aggressive expansion plans to reach more branches by 2028.

Subsidiaries

MKC Capital Markets Corp., MKC Digital Pay Inc., MKC Insurance Holdings, MKC Micro-Lending Corp.

Vision

To become the Philippines' most innovative and trusted bank, empowering every Filipino family to achieve financial freedom through technology-driven solutions.

Mission

To deliver accessible, transparent, and cutting-edge financial services that uplift communities, drive economic growth, and set new standards in Philippine banking.

📘 ACCOUNT GUIDE

How to Open a Basic Bank Account

Everything you need to know to start your financial journey with MKC Bank.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Prepare Requirements: Gather all necessary documents (listed below).
  2. Visit a Branch or Apply Online: Use the MKC Mobile App for a 5-minute digital onboarding, or visit any of our 50+ branches nationwide.
  3. Fill Out Forms: Complete the Customer Information File (CIF) and account opening forms.
  4. Initial Deposit: Fund your account with the minimum initial deposit.
  5. Receive Your Card: Get your Visa Debit Card instantly at the branch or delivered to your home within 3-5 days.

Document Requirements

  • At least one (1) valid Primary ID (e.g., Passport, UMID, Driver's License, PhilID).
  • Proof of Billing Address (e.g., Electric bill, Water bill).
  • 1x1 or 2x2 ID Picture (if applying in branch).

Basic Account Features

  • Initial Deposit: ₱500 only
  • Maintaining Balance: ₱500
  • Interest Rate: 4.0% p.a. (promotional rate for 2025)
  • Card Included: EMV Visa Contactless Debit Card
